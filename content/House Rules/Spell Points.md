# Spell Points

Rifted replaces spell slots with a **spell point pool**. Casting a spell costs points equal to its level. All spellcasting classes use this system.

> [!tip] Core Rule
> Casting a spell costs **1 point per spell level.**
> A 1st-level spell costs 1 pt. A 5th-level spell costs 5 pts.

## Calculating Your Pool

At the start of each day, your pool = **the sum of (each slot level × number of slots at that level)** from your class's official spell slot chart.

**Example — Level 5 Wizard** (slots: 4/3/2):
(4×1) + (3×2) + (2×3) = 4 + 6 + 6 = **16 points**

## Rules

- Points replenish as per the class's rules on replenishment
- Cantrips are unaffected — cast freely as normal
- You cannot cast a spell above your class's max spell level, regardless of points available

## Sorcery Points (Sorcerer Only)

Sorcery Points remain a separate resource used primarily for **Metamagic**.

Font of Magic's conversion rates are *less efficient* than spell points, so there is no exploit:

| Slot Level | Sorcery Pts to Create | Spell Pts Equivalent | Net |
| ---------- | --------------------- | -------------------- | --- |
| 1st        | 2                     | 1                    | −1  |
| 2nd        | 3                     | 2                    | −1  |
| 3rd        | 5                     | 3                    | −2  |
| 4th        | 6                     | 4                    | −2  |
| 5th        | 7                     | 5                    | −2  |

Converting sorcery points into casting is always a net loss — no house rule needed. Sorcerers will naturally use them for Metamagic only.
