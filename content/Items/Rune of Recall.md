## Description

A flat stone the size of a river pebble, warm to the touch, etched with a single glyph that pulses faintly once per day. Each Rune of Recall is attuned at creation to a specific individual — this one was tuned to [[Jonathan Jordan]]. [[Luks Borg]] carried it as an emergency retrieval measure.

## Properties

When broken, the Rune instantly teleports the bearer to the side of the individual it is attuned to, regardless of distance. The magic is one-use and consumable — the rune is destroyed upon activation.

[[Gill Bates]] broke this rune and was teleported from [[Sunhold]] to [[Jonathan Jordan]]'s location in the Collapsed Tower of the [[Sunless Citadel]].

## Significance

[[Luks Borg]] reconsidered the party's departure when he realized they had no healer. Rather than recalling the whole party or abandoning the mission, he used the rune to send [[Gill Bates]] directly in — a decisive, surgical solution that reflects Luks's approach to problems generally.
