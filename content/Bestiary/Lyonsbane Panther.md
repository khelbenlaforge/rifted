## Description

Not a creature. A curse wearing the shape of one.

The Lyonsbane Panther is what happens when [[Kelemvor]] performs an act of genuine kindness or selflessness without first demanding payment. His bloodline was cursed five generations ago by a Hag whose life his ancestor refused to save. The curse reversed itself over generations — where it once punished greed, it now punishes generosity. The form it takes is violent: the panther does not grow from him, it *erupts*. Clothes and equipment are destroyed. Blood everywhere.

Kelemvor is fully aware inside the panther. He remembers everything afterward. He has spent his life making sure no one is close enough to watch it happen.

## Ecology & Behavior

**Trigger:** Any act of genuine selflessness or kindness without receiving something of value in return. Kelemvor feels it coming — enough warning to excuse himself, strip, and find cover. What happens when he *can't* slip away is the question.

**In panther form:** Kelemvor's mind is present but cannot control his body. He is consumed by predatory instinct and pursues the nearest non-ally relentlessly. He instinctively avoids killing his companions — allies have Disadvantage on attack rolls against him, and he will not target them unless they are the *only* living creatures available.

**Ending the curse:** The panther must kill a living creature. The moment it does, Kelemvor's human form erupts from the panther (reverse Alien). He arrives prone, naked, covered in blood, and unconscious for 1 minute. He retains all memories.

**Tactical reality for the party:** You cannot reason with it. You cannot charm or frighten it. The only options are to feed it an enemy, contain it, or wait. Attacking it is possible — but difficult, and Kelemvor will remember.

## Role in the World

A hereditary curse laid by Fae magic, tied to a specific bloodline. The Lyonsbane Panther is not a creature of the natural world — it has no ecology, no habitat, no lair. It exists only as long as the curse runs its course, and then it ceases, replaced by a naked and bleeding man who has to face whatever room he just destroyed.
