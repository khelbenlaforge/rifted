## Description

A gentle, leaf-coated creature that blends seamlessly into undergrowth. Its body is draped in a living coat of foliage that rustles softly as it moves, and its wide eyes peer out from beneath a crown of tiny ferns. Druids often call them "green familiars" for their quiet, loyal demeanor.

## Ecology & Behavior

**Habitat:** Forests, jungles, and cultivated gardens. Lurkleafs form symbiotic relationships with trees, cleaning their bark of parasites and fertilizing roots with mineral-rich droppings.

**Diet:** Photosynthetic. A Lurkleaf can go up to a week without food if it spends at least four hours a day in sunlight.

**Temperament:** Shy but affectionate once trust is earned. Their presence has a calming effect on nearby creatures — a quality that makes them prized by healers and herbalists.

**Note:** [[Ember]] spotted a Lurkleaf near [[Tymora's Shrine]] in [[Sunhold]].

## Role in the World

Lurkleafs are considered living omens of forest health. When they vanish from an area, druids take it as a sign that something has gone deeply wrong with the land. Some settlements keep domesticated Lurkleafs in communal gardens as both pest control and morale.
