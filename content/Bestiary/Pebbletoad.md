## Description

A palm-sized amphibian whose body absorbs minerals from the ground, giving each Pebbletoad a unique "stone pattern" across its rough, mottled skin. When still, it is virtually indistinguishable from the rocks around it. When content, it hums softly — a sound like river pebbles clinking in current.

## Ecology & Behavior

**Habitat:** Pebbletoads dwell in caves, streambeds, and temple ruins, favoring places where moisture meets stone.

**Temperament:** Docile and skittish. At the first sign of danger, a Pebbletoad hardens into a stone form indistinguishable from an ordinary pebble. It can remain petrified for up to 24 hours, reverting only when warmed by water, flame, or gentle sunlight for one minute. Upon awakening, it emits a faint "pop" and releases harmless mineral dust that tastes faintly of salt.

**Social:** Pebbletoads gather in loose colonies. The party collected several petrified specimens before their marsh journey to [[Sunhold]].

## Role in the World

Pebbletoads are common throughout [[Corranor]]'s wilder regions. Travelers sometimes pocket petrified Pebbletoads as good-luck charms or curiosities. Druids regard them as indicators of healthy groundwater — where Pebbletoads thrive, the earth is clean.
