## Description

The vast network of tunnels, caverns, and subterranean passages that runs beneath the surface of [[Aenath]]. Dark, largely unmapped, and home to creatures that have adapted — or been warped — by permanent absence of light. It is not one place but many, connected by passages that stretch across the continent and descend to depths that few surface-dwellers have ever reached and returned from.

The Underdark of Aenath shares the fundamental character of all such places: isolation breeds strangeness, and the things that live down here have had a long time to become something other than what they were.

## Known Access Points

- **[[Sunless Citadel]]** — Tunnels in the Upper Level bore into the Underdark. [[Balsag]] became lost in these passages while searching for food for [[Calcryx]], eventually emerging on the Grove Level.
