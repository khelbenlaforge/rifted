## Description

A vast ancient ruin entombed in a deep ravine far south of [[Sunhold]], past the great marshes and into a desolate, rocky stretch of the island. The ravine is a long crack in the stone terrain, plunging far below the surface. Sunlight barely reaches the citadel's entrance — a permanent twilight clings to its upper approaches, giving the place its name.

The citadel is divided into at least two known levels:

- **Upper Level** — A contested zone of warring [[Ordahir Kobolds|Kobolds]] and [[Durbuluk Goblins|Goblins]], with tunnels that bore into the [[Underdark]]
- **Grove Level** — A deeper subterranean expanse suffused with violet bioluminescent moss. The southern reaches show signs of a catastrophic geological shift — a fault line that violently displaced the citadel's lower section westward, leaving a crack in the earth leading deeper still. Home to the druid [[Belak]] and the unnatural grove where the [[Hucrele Family|Hucrele]] children were taken.

### The Sealed Door

Deep in the Grove Level's southern reaches, past the fault line and the crack, the party discovered a massive basalt monolith — seamless black rock with no visible door frame. Hundreds of deity symbols are engraved into its surface yet move freely, some drifting slowly, others darting with purpose. As living beings approach, the symbols gravitate toward them based on divine affinity — law and protection toward paladins, primordial forces toward elemental clerics, trickster gods toward rogues.

The door is both a ward and a test. Those who fail to demonstrate understanding are rendered unconscious, their consciousness drawn into the door itself, where they must navigate a symbolic battlefield of deity echoes and prove worthy of passage. The party passed the test by guiding the warring deity factions toward unity against an overwhelming, unknowable force — an incandescent light that was neither good, evil, nor neutral, but something entirely *other*.

### The Sanctum

Beyond the Sealed Door lies the [[Sanctum of Ashardalon]] — a chamber of extraordinary cosmological significance that predates the dragon cult entirely. An illusion of [[Ashardalon]] greets entrants, dissolving only for those who passed the door's test. Only the five from Earth can perceive its true contents.

## Current Events

The party has explored deep into the Grove Level alongside companions [[Balsag]], [[Erky]], and a Velvet Vulper called [[Little Sister]]. [[Talgen Hucrele Jr.]] was found dead among the bioluminescent growth. [[Sharwyn Hucrele]], [[Sir Braford]], and [[Karakas]] remain missing, believed to be deeper within the citadel near [[Belak]].

The party discovered and passed through the Sealed Door, gaining access to the Sanctum — a hidden chamber of profound cosmological significance predating the dragon cult. They are currently studying its contents, which challenge everything they thought they understood about [[Aenath]].
