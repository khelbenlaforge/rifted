## Description

A walled fortress-city on the western shore of the Moonsea, Zhentil Keep is the seat of the [[Zhentarim]] — the Black Network. Nominally a trading city and member of the Moonsea's loose confederation of city-states, in practice it is a military and mercantile empire ruled through fear and coin.

The Keep is where [[Manshoon]] — legendary Zhentarim wizard and architect of the assault on [[Shadowdale]] — operates. He sent his battle lords south rather than committing himself directly. The decision to stay at Zhentil Keep rather than lead the field operation is not cowardice; it is strategy.
