## Description

The Temple of [[Istishia]], built over a natural underground spring in the [[Diamond District]]. Water is everywhere — channels, fountains, and serene pools create a constant, soothing murmur that drowns out the city beyond its walls. The architecture mimics the movement of waves in blue-tinted stone and glass, and soft blue-green light dances across every surface.

At the heart of the sanctuary lies a spring-fed pool with an altar that appears to float above the water, supported by a hidden pedestal beneath the surface. Ocean mosaics adorn the walls, and concentric benches invite contemplation.

## Notable Features

- **[[Blessing Pool]]** — Where visitors seek divine favor from [[Istishia]]
- **[[Divination Fountain]]** — Used for scrying and prophetic visions
- **[[Rainfall Chamber]]** — A room that simulates gentle artificial rain for meditation

## Notable Residents

- **[[Tidecaller Marin Deepcurrent]]** — Water genasi cleric of [[Istishia]] who mentors [[Gill Bates]], teaching patience, adaptability, and the philosophy that water flows rather than fights

## Current Events

[[Gill Bates]] trained here, learning to stop resisting the current and instead observe water's hidden patterns. [[Tidecaller Marin Deepcurrent]] taught him the four sects of [[Istishia]] — Sacred Sea, Watery Paths, Magnificent Storm, and Eternal Transformation — and warned that this path might make him seem detached to others.
