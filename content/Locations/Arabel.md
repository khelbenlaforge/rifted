## Description

A mercenary city in the interior of Cormyr, known for its rough-and-tumble trade culture and tolerance for adventurers, bounty hunters, and freelancers. Where fortunes are made in odd jobs, pest control, and contract work.

## Features

- Hub for mercenary work and adventurers-for-hire
- Diverse population; pragmatic attitude toward law and order
- Home base for Kelemvor, Cyric, Torm, Adon, and Midnight before the Avatar Crisis
