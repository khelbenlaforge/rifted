## Description

A temple that is less a building than a piece of the forest itself, transplanted into the heart of [[Crystal City]]'s [[Diamond District]]. Ancient trees grow through the structure, their trunks forming archways and their canopies serving as living rafters. Wood and stone blend seamlessly, with vines and moss encouraged to thrive across every surface. Open-air sections feature retractable roofs that let sunlight and rain nurture the plants within. Inside, the boundary between outdoors and indoors dissolves entirely — the floors are packed earth covered in moss and grass, and small animals roam freely throughout.

## Notable Features

- **The Altar** — A living tree stump surrounded by offerings of acorns, seeds, and carved wooden tokens
- **Healing Grove** — A garden of medicinal plants used for treating ailments
- **Wild Nursery** — A sanctuary for orphaned or injured animals
- **Ranger's Rest** — Simple bunks for rangers on hiatus between expeditions
- **Water Channels** — Streams that wind through the interior, connecting the grove to the city's natural springs

## Notable Residents

- **[[Sylvara Oakwhisper]]** — 94-year-old half-elf ranger who teaches [[Mielikki]]'s principles of balance between wilderness and civilization
- **[[Frost]]** — Sylvara's white wolf companion

## Current Events

[[Ember]] trained here under [[Sylvara Oakwhisper]], learning druidic philosophy and the balance between nature and civilization. Sylvara taught that serving [[Mielikki]] does not mean rejecting the works of mortals, but rather protecting the wild places while embracing sustainable coexistence.
