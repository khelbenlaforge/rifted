## Description

The most organized structure in [[Sunhold]] — a reinforced tent with canvas walls secured by iron locks, its shelves made from planks and barrels displaying flour, salt, tools, and whatever goods the supply caravans have delivered. Prices are chalked on slate boards, and the air is thick with the scent of leather and metal. In a settlement where every item is precious, [[Barthen's Supplies]] operates with military precision.

## Notable Residents

- **[[Elmar Barthen]]** — Lean dwarf storeowner with spectacles, who manages supplies with pragmatic efficiency
