## Description

Perched on a small rise at the edge of [[Sunhold]], this humble shrine is built from rough local materials but radiates warmth and hope. A wooden platform supports an altar carved with Tymora's smiling visage, her disk symbol inlaid with copper coins donated by miners. Holy symbols fashioned from coins, dice, and horseshoes hang from the support posts. Burning incense mingles with the fresh scent of wilderness, and the sound of pickaxes fades to silence here — a rare pocket of peace on the frontier.

## Notable Residents

- **[[Sister Garaele]]** — Half-elf cleric of Tymora who offers healing and spiritual comfort

## Current Events

[[Gill Bates]] was sent here to assist [[Sister Garaele]], who revealed that her gnome acolyte [[Erky]] had ventured south alone to rescue the Hucrele children and had not returned. The party received healing potions before departing on their own rescue mission.
