## Description

A grand courthouse-temple dedicated to [[Tyr]], the God of Justice. Impressive marble columns support a symmetrical facade, and a twenty-foot sculpture of the Scales of Justice dominates the main entrance. Inside, a central statue of [[Tyr]] stands blindfolded and armored, his right hand missing — a symbol of impartiality and the cost of true justice. The atmosphere is solemn and formal, with petitioners seeking arbitration and guards in gleaming armor maintaining order.

## Notable Features

- **Arbitration Chamber** — Where free legal counsel is provided to those in need
- **Oathstone** — A black marble pillar used for binding sacred oaths
- **Training Courtyards** — Visible through side archways, where paladins practice combat and discipline

## Notable Residents

- **[[Sir Gareth Ironhand]]** — 48-year-old human paladin, missing his left arm below the elbow (replaced with an iron prosthetic), who trains aspiring paladins in the balance of strength, wisdom, and mercy

## Current Events

[[Augustus Kennedy]] chose [[Tyr]] as his path and trained here under [[Sir Gareth Ironhand]], learning that paladin power derives from the conviction of one's oath — whether sworn to a deity, to ideals, or to people.
