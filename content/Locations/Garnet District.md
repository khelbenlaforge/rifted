## Description

The Commons District — a lively quarter of bustling bars, taverns, and humble dwellings where [[Crystal City]]'s common folk live and drink. The architecture is rustic and varied, the air thick with laughter, clinking mugs, and the mingled accents of a dozen races. Adventurers rub shoulders with dockworkers and miners fresh from the frontier.

## Notable Features

- A popular tavern where a [[Dwarven Bartender]] dispenses ale and local knowledge. He warned [[Augustus Kennedy]] about the wilds of [[Corranor]]'s mainland and mentioned his brother [[Devran]] in [[Stormberg]].
