# Sacred Sea

One of the four sects of [[Istishia]]'s faith, as taught by [[Tidecaller Marin Deepcurrent]] at the [[Tidecaller's Sanctuary]]. The Sacred Sea emphasises reverence for water in its vast, unchanging form — the ocean as the origin and destiny of all things. Its practitioners tend toward contemplation and awe, finding the divine in stillness and depth.

The other three sects are [[Watery Paths]], [[Magnificent Storm]], and [[Eternal Transformation]].
