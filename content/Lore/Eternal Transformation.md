# Eternal Transformation

One of the four sects of [[Istishia]]'s faith, as taught by [[Tidecaller Marin Deepcurrent]] at the [[Tidecaller's Sanctuary]]. The Eternal Transformation sect holds that water's defining quality is its capacity to change state — liquid, ice, vapour — while remaining essentially itself. Its teachings centre on cycles, transition, and the idea that change is not loss but continuity in a different form.

The other three sects are [[Sacred Sea]], [[Watery Paths]], and [[Magnificent Storm]].
