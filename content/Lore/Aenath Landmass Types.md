# Aenath Landmass Types

[[Aenath]]'s geography is defined by three distinct categories of landmass, each behaving differently in relation to the ocean and sky.

## Terra-Bound

Islands and continents that rest permanently at sea level. They do not rise, descend, or drift — they are fixed in place, subject only to weather and tide. Most of the world's stable agriculture and long-term settlement exists on terra-bound land. The western landmass the party sailed to aboard the [[Vanny Faire]] is terra-bound.

## Transient

Landmasses that periodically rise into the sky and descend back to the ocean, on cycles that range from regular and predictable to erratic and sudden — varying by island. [[Corranor]]'s main island is transient. Its recent descent to the ocean was celebrated across [[Crystal City]] as a significant event, ramping up dock traffic and trade.

## Sky-Bound

Formations that float permanently, never descending to sea level. These exist at elevation indefinitely, connected to the surface world by arcane-powered airships or, in rare cases, natural means.

---

The three types are not mutually exclusive in a geographic sense — a region may contain all three in proximity. Understanding which type a landmass belongs to is considered basic navigational knowledge in [[Corranor]].
