# Magnificent Storm

One of the four sects of [[Istishia]]'s faith, as taught by [[Tidecaller Marin Deepcurrent]] at the [[Tidecaller's Sanctuary]]. The Magnificent Storm sect centres on water as force — the ocean in fury, the storm surge, the flood. Its practitioners embrace [[Istishia]]'s destructive and overwhelming aspect, understanding that water does not ask permission when it moves.

The other three sects are [[Sacred Sea]], [[Watery Paths]], and [[Eternal Transformation]].
