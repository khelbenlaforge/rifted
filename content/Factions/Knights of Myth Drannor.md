## Description

A band of adventurers-turned-heroes who began as the Swords of Eveningstar and renamed themselves after formally pledging to the restoration of Myth Drannor — the ancient elven city, once the greatest center of magic in Faerûn, now a ruin haunted by demons. Their mandate is broad: defend the Dalelands, oppose tyrants, and keep the roads between good people open.

They are not an institution with a hierarchy. They are a company — a group of specific people who trust each other and have decided that doing good is worth the cost. Their methods are direct, their loyalty personal, their reputation earned fight by fight.

## Known Members

- [[Torm of the Dales]] — halfling thief, de facto social engine of the group
- [[Rathan Thentraver]] — cleric of [[Tymora]], Torm's lifelong best friend
- [[Mourngrym Amcathra]] — Lord of Shadowdale; ally and associate
- [[Storm Silverhand]] — ally; allied with but not formally a member

## Role in the Story

*Prequel vision — Avatar Crisis era.* The Knights are based in [[Shadowdale]] during the Avatar Crisis, operating in the Dalelands as the Zhentarim advance from the north. They are Shadowdale's irregular defense force alongside [[Mourngrym Amcathra]]'s official guard. The prequel party encounters Rathan and Torm in Shadowdale, and Mourngrym offers them a formal role in the town's defense.
