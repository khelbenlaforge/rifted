## Description

A wealthy noble family from [[Crystal City]] who relocated to invest in [[Sunhold]]'s frontier development. The Hucreles provide significant funding to the settlement and expect governance that reflects their status — a source of friction with the [[Miners' Council]]. They have alleged political troubles back in Crystal City that preceded their move south.

## Leadership & Members

- **[[Keldara Hucrele]]** — Matriarch. Encouraged her children's adventurous spirits; now wracked with guilt over the delegation's disappearance.
- **[[Talgen Hucrele Sr.]]** — Patriarch. Relocated alongside Keldara to oversee the family's Sunhold investments.
- **[[Sharwyn Hucrele]]** — Daughter. Missing in the [[Sunless Citadel]].
- **[[Talgen Hucrele Jr.]]** — Son. Found dead on the Grove Level.
- **[[Sir Braford]]** — Knight in service to the family. Missing in the Sunless Citadel.
- **[[Sir Garth]]** — Family guard stationed at Sunhold. Now the sole armed protector of the family in the settlement.

## Operations

The Hucreles funded and encouraged a delegation south to the [[Durbuluk Goblins]] after [[Goblin Merchants]] arrived in Sunhold with healing fruit and an invitation. The delegation — including both children and their retainers — failed to return within the expected ten days. Twenty-four days passed with no word before the [[Adamantine Guild]] took up the search.

**Reward posted:** 500 gold per child returned alive · 200 gold per signet ring if found deceased.

## Current Status

In crisis. [[Talgen Hucrele Jr.]] confirmed dead. [[Sharwyn Hucrele]] and [[Sir Braford]] remain missing. [[Sir Garth]] holds the family's position at [[Sunhold]] alone.
