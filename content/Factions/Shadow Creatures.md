## Description

Malevolent beings of darkness and chaos that manifest as indistinct shadowy figures, their true forms obscured. They embody an existential threat to [[Corranor]] — not merely dangerous creatures, but harbingers of something deeper and more troubling.

## Operations

The Shadow Creatures emerged during High Harvestide of Year 222, coinciding with the arrival of the five Earth newcomers atop the [[Jade Spire]]. Whether this timing is coincidental or connected remains unknown. Their attacks have focused on the Jade Spire itself, rendering its upper levels unsafe and forcing evacuations.

## Current Status

An ongoing and escalating threat. Their attacks have caused severe staffing shortages in the [[Adamantine Guild]], as resources are diverted to confront or flee from them. Their origins, motivations, and the nature of their connection to the newcomers' arrival are among the campaign's central mysteries.
