## Description

A civilized and organized faction of goblins from the unexplored regions south of [[Sunhold]], beyond the vast marshes. Unlike the [[Goblin Bandits]], these goblins wear proper clothing, speak Common fluently, and travel with carts laden with goods. Their most notable offering is the [[Goblin Fruit]] — a magical red fruit said to heal any ailment.

## Operations

They visited [[Sunhold]] two weeks before the Hucrele children disappeared, bringing the healing fruit and inviting a delegation to visit their stronghold past the marshes. Their trade overtures appear genuine, but the disappearance of everyone who has followed them south casts a dark shadow over their intentions.

## Current Status

The delegation that went south — [[Sharwyn]], [[Talgen Hucrele Jr.]], [[Sir Braford]], and [[Karakas]] — has been missing for roughly fourteen days. [[Erky]], who went after them alone, has also not returned. The party is currently investigating.
