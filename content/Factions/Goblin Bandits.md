## Description

A marauding band of 20-30 goblins operating in the wilderness surrounding [[Sunhold]]. Unlike the civilized [[Goblin Merchants]], these are opportunistic raiders — a mix of local inhabitants and immigrants from [[Corranor]] who target travelers and supply caravans along the roads.

## Operations

They favor ambush tactics, striking at isolated wagons and travelers on the rural paths leading to [[Sunhold]]. Marks of their attacks — wrecked wagons, scattered goods — are a common sight. Their frequent raids have created instability and suspicion toward all goblins in the region.

## Current Status

The party encountered a battle site and was later ambushed by Goblin Bandits on the road to [[Sunhold]]. They fought their way through, though [[Lea]] nearly fell. Roughly 20-30 footprints led into the wilderness, but the party declined to pursue.
