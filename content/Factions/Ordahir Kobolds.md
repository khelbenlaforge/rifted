## Description

The Ordahir Kobolds are a colony occupying the southern and eastern sections of the [[Sunless Citadel]]'s Upper Level. They came to the citadel for its ancient dragon-worship heritage — the carved imagery throughout the structure, the legacy of [[Ashardalon]]'s cult — and have organized their identity around that connection.

The colony is currently fractured. On the surface, [[Yusdrayl]] rules from a salvaged stone throne. Beneath that surface, [[Ragh]] commands the western military post and the colony's true believers, and has been quietly working toward her removal.

## Leadership & Members

- **[[Yusdrayl]]** — Queen; pragmatic, politically astute, uses dragon mythology as governing rhetoric rather than genuine faith
- **[[Ragh]]** — Chief military officer; true dragon devotee; secretly orchestrated [[Calcryx]]'s kidnapping to undermine Yusdrayl
- **[[Meepo]]** — Former keeper of [[Calcryx]]; blamed by the colony for the wyrmling's loss; socially marginalized

**Internal factions:**
- **Yusdrayl's camp** — pragmatists, politically motivated, see the wyrmling as a symbol
- **Ragh's camp** — traditionalists, genuine believers in dragon heritage, growing in discontent

## Operations

The Ordahir Kobolds hold their territory against the [[Durbuluk Goblins]] to the north and against Underdark threats from the west. Ragh's post guards the western tunnels. The colony has been in a state of internal tension since the goblins stole [[Calcryx]] — a riot to retrieve the wyrmling by force is days away.

The colony does not have access to [[Belak]]'s magic fruit supply — a deal the [[Durbuluk Goblins]] hold exclusively, which [[Yusdrayl]] envies.

## Current Status

**Fracturing.** The kidnapping of [[Calcryx]] has exposed the fault line between Yusdrayl's faction and Ragh's. Ragh has already brokered the kidnapping with [[Durnn]] and is preparing an uprising. Yusdrayl does not know. The party has been offered competing deals by both leaders.
