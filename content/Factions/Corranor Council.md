## Description

The ruling body of the [[Magocracy of Corranor]], comprising seven archmages who govern from the [[Jade Spire]]. The council oversees magical research, territorial expansion, and the defense of [[Corranor]]'s scattered landmasses. They are currently grappling with the emergence of [[Shadow Creatures]] at the Jade Spire and the mystery of five individuals who appeared from Earth during what may have been a botched ritual.

## Leadership & Members

- **[[Garm]]** — Orc wizard, 18th level; the most prominently active member, specializing in dimensional magic
- Six other archmages (names not yet established)

## Operations

The council directs arcane-powered airship expansion, manages relations with distant continents, and funds organizations like the [[Adamantine Guild]]. They have been criticized for failing to deliver promised supplies to the guild.

## Current Status

Under strain. The [[Shadow Creatures]] threat has diverted resources, and [[Garm]]'s suspicion that his assistant [[Layla]] triggered the ritual bringing the newcomers adds complexity to their deliberations.
