## Description

A seagoing vessel equipped with conventional sails but propelled primarily by arcane magic, allowing it to maintain reliable schedules regardless of wind conditions. The Vanny Faire serves as a vital supply link between [[Crystal City]] and the frontier settlements of [[Corranor]]'s western landmasses, carrying goods, animals, and passengers to outposts like [[Sunhold]].
