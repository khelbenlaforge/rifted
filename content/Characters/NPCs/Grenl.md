## Appearance

Old by goblin reckoning. She carries the deliberate stillness of someone who has learned that visible emotion is a liability. Her eyes do the work her face will not — reading rooms, measuring risks, settling on the least-dangerous path forward in time that would embarrass a younger creature.

## Personality

Grenl survived the transition of power that killed every other elder of the [[Durbuluk Goblins]] by making one clear calculation: opposing [[Durnn]] would accomplish nothing except her death. She chose to live. This is not cowardice — it is a form of discipline that requires watching people you might have protected get removed, and accepting that your survival has to be worth something.

She disapproves of the war with the [[Ordahir Kobolds]]. She believes Durnn's aggressive posture serves no one and grinds both factions toward ruin. She does not say this aloud. She keeps her opinions private because she has had to, for long enough that the habit is now structural.

She is not warm. She is careful. The distinction matters.

## Background

Grenl was one of several Durbuluk elders when [[Durnn]] rose to power by killing his predecessors. She alone survived — by not opposing him. She is now the only surviving elder, keeping what stability the colony has through quiet competence and strategic silence.

When the party arrived claiming alliance with [[Ragh]] and [[Durnn]] and presented letters as evidence, she admitted them — not because she fully believed them, but because escalation served no purpose she could identify. She led them to [[Calcryx]]'s cell.
