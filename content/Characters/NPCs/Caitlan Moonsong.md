## Appearance

Young, slim, blond, barefoot. She wore dark robes and moved with a grace that didn't quite match her apparent age. Her eyes were dark and serious. She looked determined and hopeful in a combination that read as desperation controlled with dignity.

In her final moments, [[Mystra]] fully occupied her: her hair moved as if alive, her eyes and skin and pendant all blazed with blue-white radiance. The body of Caitlan Moonsong was the vessel through which [[Mystra]] walked to her death.

## Background

An orphan. She described herself as the servant of "a great worker of magic" — she was, in fact, the intended avatar of [[Mystra]], the Goddess of Magic. She had walked far and long to find people willing to rescue her mistress from Castle Kilgrave, where [[Mystra]]'s avatar had been captured and chained by [[Bane]].

She was operated under something like a *geas* — when [[Mystra]]'s suffering peaked, Caitlan's body responded with fever, visible veins, and physical collapse. She sent the party ahead and promised to follow.

She made it to Castle Kilgrave just as the illusions lifted.

## What Happened

When Caitlan touched the chained essence of [[Mystra]], the goddess took her as an avatar — completing the apotheosis that had always been Caitlan's purpose. Mystra used this body to confront [[Helm]] at the celestial staircase. Helm destroyed her.

The explosion leveled half a mile of landscape. [[Midnight]] was left standing at the center of the ruins, with the pendant at her feet.

What became of the part of Caitlan that was not [[Mystra]] is unknown. Some essences survive a god's destruction. Most do not.

## Lore Note

A canonical character from the Forgotten Realms **Avatar Series** (The Times of Trouble). No PC inhabited her body — she was an NPC throughout.
