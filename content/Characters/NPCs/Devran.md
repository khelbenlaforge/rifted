## Description
Little is recorded of [[Devran]]'s appearance or manner, save that he is a dwarf of [[Stormberg]], the northern port settlement. He is known primarily through the words of his brother, the [[Dwarven Bartender]] of the [[Garnet District]], who references him when speaking of the wilds beyond the city walls.

## Background
[[Devran]] resides in [[Stormberg]], a settlement on the northern coast. His brother, a bartender in [[Crystal City]]'s [[Garnet District]], often shares secondhand knowledge of the [[Corranor]] main island's wilderness -- knowledge that likely originates from [[Devran]]'s experiences in the more remote reaches of the land. He was mentioned in conversation with [[Augustus Kennedy]], suggesting he may serve as a useful contact for those venturing northward.
