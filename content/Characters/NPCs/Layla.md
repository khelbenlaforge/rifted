## Description
Little is known of Layla's appearance, but her position speaks volumes. As assistant to [[Garm]], an orc wizard seated on the [[Corranor Council]], she operates at the heart of the magical research conducted within the [[Jade Spire]]. She possesses knowledge of rituals and arcane theory sufficient to serve one of the Council's most formidable minds.

## Background
Layla works alongside [[Garm]] in the [[Jade Spire]] of [[Crystal]], where the Council oversees magical research and experimentation. She is suspected of having accidentally triggered a ritual that tore five individuals from Earth and deposited them in [[Aenath]] -- the event that set the entire campaign in motion. [[Garm]] himself expressed surprise and confusion at the newcomers' arrival and has theorized that Layla's involvement, whether intentional or accidental, was the catalyst.

The full truth of what happened remains unclear, and Layla's role in the summoning is still under investigation.
