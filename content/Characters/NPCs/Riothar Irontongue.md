## Description

Guardscaptain of Shadowdale's western gate. Polite, even-tempered, and shrewd. Asks names and business as a matter of form — and reads the answers with practiced attention. Not a man who is fooled easily, but also not a man who makes scenes.

## Role in the Story

*Prequel vision — Avatar Crisis era.* First official point of contact when the prequel party arrives at Shadowdale. He directs them to the [[Old Skull Inn]] and assigns [[Hawksguard]] as escort, citing armed soldiers in the area.

He is running a quiet war. His morning briefings with [[Selmavra Elsree]] are his mechanism for staying ahead of the Zhentarim advance — he names the [[Zhentarim]] directly, something most locals are still dancing around. When a guard reports that the north road has gone quiet, Riothar doesn't answer. He just listens, and adds it to the picture he is building.

A good guardscaptain. Shadowdale is lucky to have him.
