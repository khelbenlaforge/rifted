## Description
Karakas is a seasoned woodsman and the only ranger of note in [[Sunhold]]. His bearing speaks of long years spent reading trails, tracking game, and navigating the wilds beyond the settlement's borders. Practical and self-reliant, he is the sort of man settlers turn to when the wilderness presses too close.

## Background
When [[Talgen Hucrele Sr.]] needed a guide for his children's delegation to the [[Goblin Merchants]], Karakas was the natural choice -- the sole woodsman in [[Sunhold]] with the skill to lead a party through the surrounding marshes. He departed alongside [[Sir Braford]], [[Sharwyn]], and [[Talgen Hucrele Jr.]] to meet the goblins at their stronghold beyond the wetlands. The delegation was expected to return within ten days.

Twenty-four days have now passed with no word. Karakas, along with the rest of the delegation, is missing. The [[Hucrele Family]] has posted a reward for their safe return, and the [[Adamantine Guild]] has taken up the search.

## Relationships
