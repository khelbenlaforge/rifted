## Appearance

Silver hair — not grey, silver. It has always been that way; the visible mark of a Chosen. Tall and athletic, appearing to be in her mid-30s though she has been alive for over four centuries. Carries a longsword with the ease of someone who learned to use it before most living people were born. Nothing about her clothing announces what she is, which is exactly the point.

## Personality

Warm, direct, unhurried. Speaks to everyone as though they are worth her full attention — because they are. She has outlived enough crises to know that underestimating people is a waste of time. Occasionally wry. Wears extraordinary power lightly. One of the founders of the [[Harpers]], and has been shaping events from the edges of history for centuries.

## Background

One of the Seven Sisters — all daughters of [[Mystra]] by mortal men, all Chosen. She channels the Weave directly through a power called Silverfire: pure blue-silver magical energy that is the Weave made manifest. She can sense its disruptions the way most people sense changes in the weather — and the Time of Troubles has been like trying to breathe smoke.

She has lived in [[Shadowdale]] long enough to be part of its landscape. She is here when the prequel party arrives because she came to help [[Elminster]].

## The Harper Web

A founding Harper. She follows Harper doctrine absolutely: agents are never identified in the field, because anonymity is the network's strength. She knows both [[Torm of the Dales]] and [[Selmavra Elsree]] are Harpers. She will not reveal them.

## Role in the Story

Present at [[Elminster]]'s Tower when the prequel party arrives (Event 3, Session 14). Her role this session is to notice things and say almost nothing — which is more unsettling than it sounds.

She senses [[Midnight]]'s pendant the moment the party is within range. The shard of [[Mystra]]'s broken power radiates to her like a beacon. She catches Midnight's eye during Elminster's speech: a look that says *I know what you're carrying. So does it.*

### Features

- **Extra Attack** — Attack twice when taking the Attack action; or replace one attack with a cantrip (action cast time).
- **Combat Inspiration** — Creatures with your Bardic Inspiration die can use a Reaction to add the die to their AC against a hit, or to a damage roll after hitting.
- **Expertise** — Double PB in Performance, Persuasion, History, and Insight.
- **Jack of All Trades** — Add +2 to non-proficient skill checks.
- **Font of Inspiration** — Regain all Bardic Inspiration uses on Short or Long Rest; spend a spell slot (no action) to regain 1 use.
- **Magical Secrets** — Chain Lightning and Disintegrate prepared from Wizard spell list.
- **Chosen of Mystra** — Darkvision 60 ft. · Truesight 30 ft. · Resistance: fire, lightning · Immune: Charmed, Frightened · Weave Sense: auto-sense magical effects and other Chosen within 60 ft.
- **Chosen Resilience** — At the start of her turn while below half HP, she regains 10 HP.
- **Silverfire** — Once per turn, when Storm hits with an attack or deals spell damage, she adds 3d6 radiant damage.
