## Description

A large grey wolf with primal markings that shimmer faintly in low light — the telltale sign of a Primal Companion, a beast bound to a ranger through magical attunement rather than simple training. One ear is permanently bent, the cartilage split by an old scar. She moves quietly for something her size.

Vesper was found in a Zhentarim supply camp, half-starved and locked in a cage. [[Selmavra Elsree]] picked the lock herself. She has never tried to leave.

The Erinyes, on one of her spite-visits, once remarked that the wolf reminded her of Selmavra — *"small, stubborn, and annoyingly principled."* Selmavra chose not to respond to this. Vesper growled at the devil. The Erinyes seemed almost pleased.

## Background

Wolves are not uncommon in the Dalelands forests, but Vesper was never an ordinary wolf — or she stopped being one when Selmavra's bond took hold. She understands intent more than command, moving before she is asked, positioning herself between Selmavra and whatever she is looking at. She has an unsettling habit of sitting very still and watching people she does not trust, without blinking, for extended periods.

She tolerates the rest of the Harper unit. She does not like sudden movements.
