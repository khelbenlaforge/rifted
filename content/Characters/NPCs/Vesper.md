## Description

A large grey wolf with primal markings that shimmer faintly in low light — the telltale sign of a Primal Companion, a beast bound to a ranger through magical attunement rather than simple training. One ear is permanently bent, the cartilage split by an old scar. He moves quietly for something his size.

Vesper was found injured in the hands of merchants selling animals as pets or slaves. [[Selmavra Elsree]] got him out. He has never tried to leave.

The Erinyes, on one of her spite-visits, once remarked that the wolf reminded her of Selmavra — *"small, stubborn, and annoyingly principled."* Selmavra chose not to respond to this. Vesper growled at the devil. The Erinyes seemed almost pleased.

## Background

Wolves are not uncommon in the Dalelands forests, but Vesper was never an ordinary wolf — or he stopped being one when Selmavra's bond took hold. He understands intent more than command, moving before he is asked, positioning himself between Selmavra and whatever she is looking at. He has an unsettling habit of sitting very still and watching people he does not trust, without blinking, for extended periods.

He tolerates the rest of the Harper unit. He does not like sudden movements.

The guards at the Shadowdale post have accepted him as a permanent fixture. Some of them feed him treats.

In Session 14, Vesper fought in his first combat — the Zhentarim ambush at the Old Skull Inn courtyard. He took significant damage and spent most of the fight protecting the stable boy.
