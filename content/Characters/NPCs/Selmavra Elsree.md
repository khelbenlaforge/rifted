## Description

Lean and quiet, with dark hair and silver at the temples — unusual for a Wood Elf still in her prime, and she never explains it. Moves like someone used to not being heard. [[Vesper]] is almost always within arm's reach. A Harper pin sits hidden beneath her collar.

Commander of the Harper Skirmish unit stationed at [[Shadowdale]] during the Time of Troubles. Canon figure from the Avatar Crisis — listed in the defending forces at Shadowdale, originally of considerably higher standing. Downscaled here for the prequel vision.

## Personality

Low and measured in the field. Efficient with words where most people use twice as many. Softens noticeably around animals — something shifts in her posture, her pace, even her voice.

She rescues first and reasons later. It is reflex, not calculation. She has done it so many times the instinct has calcified into identity.

The near-death encounter with the Erinyes did not make her fearful. It made her *interesting* to a devil, which unsettles her far more than fear ever could.

## Background

Selmavra has been a Harper long enough that the organization feels less like a cause and more like a second skin. She runs the skirmish unit at Shadowdale with the quiet authority of someone who has already survived things she doesn't talk about.

[[Torm of the Dales]] is also a Harper. She knows it. He knows it. Neither will say so — not because of anything between them, but because that is simply how the Harpers operate. Agents never identify each other in the field. The network's strength is that it can be *everywhere* because no one knows who belongs to it. Selmavra has done this a hundred times with people she knows well. The trick is not to be too convincing.
