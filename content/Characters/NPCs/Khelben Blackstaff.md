## Background

One of the two most powerful mages in Faerûn — the other being [[Elminster]]. Khelben Arunsun, known as Khelben Blackstaff, is the Archmage of Waterdeep and a quiet power behind Faerûnian affairs. Where Elminster operates through counsel and wandering, Khelben operates through carefully maintained institutional influence.

He is a **Chosen of [[Mystra]]** — one of her most powerful mortal instruments, carrying a fragment of the Weave directly. The silver fire runs in him as it does in [[Elminster]] and the Seven Sisters. His fragment survived Mystra's death at [[Helm]]'s hands; what he does with it in the absence of the goddess who gave it to him is his own question to answer.

## The Blackstaff Legacy

Khelben's son is **[[Corran Blackstaff]]** — a detail the party learned through a History check nat 20 during the prequel vision. Corran would go on to become the founding archmage of [[Aenath]], sacrificing himself 222 years ago to stabilize the new world after the [[The Awakening|Awakening]].

This connection is cosmologically significant: the blood of one of Faerûn's greatest mages is the foundational stone of Aenath's arcane history. The party carries this knowledge with them — no one in Aenath should know it.
