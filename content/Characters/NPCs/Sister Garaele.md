## Description
Sister Garaele is a half-elf cleric who tends [[Tymora's Shrine]] at the edge of [[Sunhold]]. She wears simple robes and carries a silver disk bearing [[Tymora]]'s smiling face as her holy symbol. Her warm, optimistic smile rarely falters -- she holds fast to the belief that people are fundamentally good and that fortune favours those bold enough to act.

## Background
Garaele maintains [[Tymora's Shrine]], a modest structure set on a rise at the settlement's edge. Its altar is carved with [[Tymora]]'s visage and covered in coins and tokens of luck left by hopeful settlers. In a frontier community beset by danger, the shrine offers a fragile sanctuary of peace and hope.

She provides healing potions to those in need and has offered her aid to the [[Adamantine Guild]] party. She mentioned that her gnome acolyte, [[Erky]], departed south to search for the missing Hucrele children on his own but has not returned -- adding yet another name to the list of the lost. Garaele's faith teaches that Lady Luck smiles on the bold, and she believes the party may be the ones to restore hope to [[Sunhold]].
