## Appearance

A fox-like creature with midnight blue velvety fur and multiple tails — the kind of creature that belongs in a fae glade rather than a goblin dungeon. She moves with quiet, watchful grace and responds to address in Sylvan.

## Personality

Cautious and calculating at first, Little Sister spent her early time with the party observing rather than committing. Over time — particularly after descending to the Grove Level — something shifted. She began choosing the party rather than simply tolerating them, and by the time of the Sanctum she was in the fight properly, engaged and present in a way that felt deliberate.

## Background

Found caged in [[Durbuluk Goblins]] territory within the [[Sunless Citadel]], held alongside the gnome acolyte [[Erky]]. The party persuaded [[Grenl]] to release both. Her name was given rather than chosen — whether by the goblins or by circumstance is unclear. She answers to it regardless.

## Role in the Story

A companion who joined the party in Session 10 and has since become quietly integral to it. Like [[Balsag]] and [[Erky]], she cannot perceive the [[Sanctum]]'s true contents — only the Ashardalon illusion and the ancient elven architecture. Why the Sanctum reveals itself only to the five from Earth remains an open question.
