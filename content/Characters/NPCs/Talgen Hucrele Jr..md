## Description
Talgen Hucrele Jr. is a young nobleman with bright eyes and an adventurer's restless energy. He dresses in the fine clothing expected of his station but carries himself with the confidence of someone eager to prove he is more than his family name. His bravery borders on recklessness -- a quality his parents both admire and fear.

## Background
The son of [[Talgen Hucrele Sr.]] and [[Keldara Hucrele]], Talgen grew up in [[Crystal]] surrounded by privilege but yearned for something beyond a sheltered existence. When the family relocated to [[Sunhold]], the frontier offered exactly the kind of adventure he craved. The arrival of [[Goblin Merchants]] bearing a magical healing fruit and an invitation to their stronghold was an opportunity he refused to let pass.

He joined the delegation alongside his sister [[Sharwyn]], escorted by [[Sir Braford]] and [[Karakas]]. The group was expected back within ten days. Twenty-four days have passed without any word. His parents have offered a reward of 500 gold pieces for each child returned alive, or 200 gold for their signet rings if they are found dead, along with a family heirloom as additional incentive. The [[Adamantine Guild]] has taken up the search, following tracks into a subterranean fortress south of [[Sunhold]] where signs of goblin conflict and necromantic magic have been discovered.
