## Description

A sharp-eyed human woman whose practical yet quality clothing speaks to both function and status. Multiple silver rings adorn her fingers, hinting at wealth and connections that extend beyond [[Sunhold]]'s rough frontier. Halia carries herself with the calculating poise of someone who sees opportunity where others see chaos.

## Background

An ambitious merchant intent on gaining power within [[Sunhold]] and expanding her Consortium's influence. She profits from the settlement's instability while positioning herself as a voice of pragmatic leadership. At the [[Council Tent]], she sits alongside [[Harbin Wester]] and the [[Miners' Council]], her presence underscoring her significant role in the local economy and politics. "The council means well, but this settlement needs real leadership," she has been heard to say. "People who get things done."

## Relationships

- **[[Harbin Wester]]** — Works alongside him on the council, though she clearly views his leadership as insufficient
- **[[Miners' Council]]** — A key figure in Sunhold's economic and political landscape
