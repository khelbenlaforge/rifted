## Description

A middle-aged dwarf with a well-maintained beard braided with copper wire, perpetually damp with nervous sweat. Harbin dresses in typical miner's clothing and carries the burdened look of a man whose responsibilities have outgrown his capacity. He means well but is clearly overwhelmed by the demands of frontier governance.

## Background

Harbin serves as townmaster of [[Sunhold]] and leader of the [[Miners' Council]]. He was among the original prospectors who discovered the copper, silver, and iron veins in the caldera that led to the settlement's founding. When [[Keldara Hucrele]] publicly confronted him over her missing children, he struggled to assert authority, insisting that frontier life demanded different measures than noble expectations. Despite the tension, he acknowledges the Hucreles' financial contributions and their guards' role in settlement security.

Harbin welcomed the [[Adamantine Guild]]'s arrival with visible relief, escorting the party to [[Barthen's Supplies]] and later convening a council meeting to brief them on Sunhold's challenges. He tasked the guild members with safety, exploration, and community outreach — clearly eager to share the burden of leadership.
