## Appearance

Meepo is the color of old copper, small even by kobold standards, with round anxious eyes that dart toward exits before they settle on faces. He carries himself like a creature permanently braced for a blow that never quite lands — shoulders hunched, tail low, always slightly sideways. His green-cloth sleeping area was once well-tended; now it's just the place he waits.

## Personality

Meepo is not timid — he is *undone*. There is a difference. He was once, by all evidence, a kobold who knew his purpose: keeper and companion to the wyrmling [[Calcryx]], raised from hatching and given into his care. When the [[Durbuluk Goblins]] took Calcryx, they didn't just steal a dragon — they took the thing that organized Meepo's entire sense of self.

He is desperately sincere. He welcomes strangers with the warmth of someone who has run out of familiar faces to turn to. He does not perform loyalty; he is loyal, in the way of someone who no longer has enough left to waste on pretense.

## Background

Meepo was assigned as Calcryx's keeper shortly after the wyrmling hatched — a mark of trust from the colony's leadership. He took the responsibility seriously. The [[Ordahir Kobolds]] who still honor ancient dragon heritage found his devotion worthy; those who see the wyrmling as merely a political asset found him inconveniently earnest.

When the goblins raided and took Calcryx, the colony's anger needed a target. Meepo was the easiest one available. He hasn't been told he's forgiven, because he hasn't been.

## Role in the Story

- First kobold contact for the party — found in a ruined western room in a state of distress
- Meepo guided the party east to [[Yusdrayl]]'s throne room
- Guided the party northeast through unclaimed sections of the Upper Level toward goblin territory — leading them through traps and the rat warren where [[Karakas]]'s body was found
- Left behind in the rat room (northeast, open to the rift) when the party crossed into [[Durbuluk Goblins]] territory — left with food and water; too dangerous to bring a kobold into the goblin colony
- [[Calcryx]] was told of Meepo's location and expressed a soft spot for him — they may yet find each other
- He occupies a strange position: blamed by [[Yusdrayl]]'s faction, ignored by [[Ragh]]'s, devoted to a dragon neither side truly cares about as an individual
