## Description

A gnome of small stature, as is typical of his race. What is known is his devotion: he serves as an acolyte of [[Tymora's Shrine]] under [[Sister Garaele]], and his willingness to venture into danger on behalf of others speaks to a courage that belies his size.

## Background

[[Erky]] served faithfully at [[Tymora's Shrine]] in [[Sunhold]] under the guidance of [[Sister Garaele]]. When the Hucrele children went missing, he volunteered to travel south in search of them — a brave act for an acolyte more accustomed to prayer than peril.

He was captured within the [[Sunless Citadel]] but survived his ordeal. The party found and rescued him during their exploration of the upper levels.

## Rescue

The party found [[Erky]] alive within the [[Sunless Citadel]] and freed him during their exploration of the upper levels. Despite his captivity, his spirits recovered quickly — perhaps too quickly, judging by his semi-inebriated state during recent explorations of the Grove Level. He now travels with the party alongside the bugbear [[Balsag]] and a Velvet Vulper called [[Little Sister]], providing healing support and an irrepressible cheerfulness that cuts through the citadel's gloom.
