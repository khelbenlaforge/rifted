## Description
A gnome of small stature, as is typical of his race. Beyond that, little is known of [[Erky]]'s appearance. What is known is his devotion: he serves as an acolyte of [[Tymora's Shrine]] under [[Sister Garaele]], and his willingness to venture into danger on behalf of others speaks to a courage that belies his size.

## Background
[[Erky]] served faithfully at [[Tymora's Shrine]] in [[Sunhold]] under the guidance of [[Sister Garaele]]. When the Hucrele children went missing, he volunteered to travel south in search of them -- a brave act for an acolyte more accustomed to prayer than peril.

He has not returned.

[[Sister Garaele]] has expressed deep concern for his well-being, and his disappearance only adds to the mounting list of troubles facing [[Sunhold]]. As a trained acolyte, [[Erky]] possesses knowledge of healing that could prove invaluable -- if he yet lives. The dangers of the territory he ventured into suggest his situation is dire.
