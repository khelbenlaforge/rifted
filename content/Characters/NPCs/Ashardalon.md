## Description

An ancient dragon of legendary power whose shadow lingers in the deepest chambers of the [[Sunless Citadel]]. Whether Ashardalon still lives, sleeps, or has long since passed from the world is unknown — but the cult that worshipped the great wyrm built their sanctum in these halls, and the echoes of that devotion remain.

## Legacy

The [[Sunless Citadel]] was once home to a dragon cult devoted to Ashardalon. The cult's most significant legacy is the Sanctum hidden behind the Sealed Door on the Grove Level — though the Sanctum's true contents predate the cult and suggest that Ashardalon's followers built atop something far older than their faith.

An illusory statue of the great dragon guards the Sanctum's entrance, dissolving only for those who pass the door's divine test. The relationship between the dragon cult and the chamber's cosmological significance — statuary of [[Mystra]], [[Gond]], [[Bahamut]], [[Takhisis]], and the four primordial elemental gods arranged around a model of [[Aenath]] — raises profound questions about what drew the cult here in the first place.

## Relationships

- Worshipped by a dragon cult that once inhabited the [[Sunless Citadel]]
- Connected to [[Bahamut]] and [[Takhisis]] through the Sanctum's statuary, though the nature of this connection is unclear
