## Appearance

Mid-forties, grey-haired, regally beautiful. There is nothing motherly about her in a soft sense — she is motherly the way a stone wall is sheltering. She moves through the Old Skull Inn like she owns it, which she does, and like she has seen worse than whatever you are about to do, which she has.

## Personality

Hard to scare. Very good at healing arts. A prodigious drinker who appears completely sober — a skill she considers a professional requirement. Neutral Good in the particular way of people who have decided that being good is practical as well as right.

She reads guests accurately and quickly. She's not cruel about it; she just knows what she's working with.

## Background

She has run the Old Skull Inn in [[Shadowdale]] for long enough that she is part of its character. Merchants, adventurers, knights, and refugees have all come through her door. She has patched up the wounded, talked down the violent, and served drinks to people who were about to die without telling them so. She knows [[Mourngrym Amcathra]] well, and he trusts her judgment.

## Role in the Story

The prequel party meets Jhaele at the Old Skull Inn (Event 2, Session 14). She will put the party up at [[Mourngrym Amcathra|Mourngrym]]'s expense if they agree to help defend the dale. She knows where to find [[Elminster]].

After the courtyard encounter — the panther, the blood, the Zhentarim — she will want answers. She will not throw the party out. She will hand someone a cloth and wait.
