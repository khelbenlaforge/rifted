## Description

A bugbear of the [[Sunless Citadel]] who has joined the party as a companion during their exploration of the Grove Level.

## Background

Encountered within the citadel's depths, Balsag allied with the party under circumstances not yet fully recorded. He now travels alongside [[Erky]], [[Little Sister]], and the five PCs as they press deeper into the Grove Level.

## Relationships

- Traveling companion of the party
- Fellow companion alongside [[Erky]] and [[Little Sister]] (Velvet Vulper)
