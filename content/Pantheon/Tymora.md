## Description

Tymora is the goddess of luck, fortune, and the bold gamble taken with a grin. She does not reward caution — she rewards audacity. Her faithful understand that the coin doesn't land on heads because you're careful; it lands on heads because you threw it. Tymora loves those who press their luck and she punishes, gently but firmly, those who refuse to try.

She is depicted as a laughing young woman tossing a coin, light on her feet, never standing still for long. Her shrines appear at crossroads, in gambling houses, and at the doors of adventurers setting out for the first time.

## Tenets

- Dare. The unlucky are not those who fail — they are those who never try.
- Fortune favours the bold; timidity is the only true bad luck.
- Celebrate luck when it comes; accept misfortune without bitterness. Both are hers to give.
- Help others take their chances. A pushed door is worth more than a prayer.

## Followers & Influence

Adventurers, gamblers, travelers, and anyone whose life turns on moments of chance. [[Tymora's Shrine]] in [[Crystal City]] serves those seeking favour before a risky venture.
