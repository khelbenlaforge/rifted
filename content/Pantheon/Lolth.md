## Description

Lolth is the goddess of spiders, darkness, and the drow — a deity of pure self-serving chaos wrapped in the language of divine mandate. She demands absolute devotion and rewards betrayal, because betrayal is how her faithful prove they are strong enough to deserve her attention. Her church is a hierarchy designed to collapse; everyone in it is plotting against everyone above them, exactly as she intends.

## Tenets

- Survive. Dominate. Betray upward.
- Spiders do not ask permission. Neither should you.
- Weakness in any form is an invitation to be consumed.

## Followers & Influence

The drow and those who dwell in the deep dark. Her influence in [[Aenath]] is minimal — she has not established significant presence in the world as it currently stands.
