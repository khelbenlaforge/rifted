## Description
Waukeen is the goddess of wealth, trade, and the restless energy of commerce. She concerns herself with anything that increases the flow of coin — new trade routes, clever inventions, shifting fashions, daring ventures on both sides of the law. The Coinmaiden does not distinguish between a merchant prince and a cunning smuggler; both move goods, both create opportunity, and both serve the engine of prosperity that she believes drives civilisation forward.

Yet Waukeen is no patron of misers. She smiles upon the industrious and the generous, and frowns upon those who hoard wealth while others struggle. Her faithful are taught that gold sitting idle in a vault is gold wasted — true prosperity circulates, lifting the fortunes of all it touches. Competition, not monopoly, is the mechanism of progress, and those who would strangle trade for selfish gain earn Waukeen's displeasure.

## Tenets
- Wealth exists to flow, not to stagnate. Spend, invest, and circulate.
- Create opportunity for all; oppose monopolies and those who hoard at others' expense.
- Set aside a tithe of ten percent of your profits — not for a temple, but to help a struggling business, finance a new endeavour, or, failing all else, spend on frivolous enjoyment.
- Embrace innovation and change; new ideas are the truest currency.
- Trade is the lifeblood of civilisation. Protect it, expand it, and never let it dry up.

## Followers & Influence
Waukeen's most ardent worshippers include shopkeepers, members of trading costers, wealthy merchants, caravan guides, itinerant peddlers, moneychangers, and smugglers. Her temples resemble guildhalls and frequently serve as meeting places for trade consortiums. Priests bearing her holy symbol find themselves welcome in towns and cities across [[Corranor]], for where Waukeen's faithful go, commerce follows.

In [[Crystal City]], Waukeen's coin sits at the centre of the [[Adamantine Guild]]'s emblem, flanked by the symbols of [[Oghma]] and [[Helm]]. The Guild was founded in accordance with her principles of advancement and prosperity, and her influence shapes its mission to support adventurers and provide resources for those seeking to improve themselves and their communities. As the Guild faces challenges of understaffing and shadow creature threats, Waukeen's guidance toward resourcefulness and investment in people has never been more critical.
