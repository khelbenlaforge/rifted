## Description
Mielikki is the goddess of forests, rangers, and the delicate balance between wilderness and civilisation. She is often envisioned as a serene figure wreathed in living green, surrounded by the flora and fauna she tends with tireless devotion. Where [[Silvanus]] governs the wild in its rawest, most unforgiving form, Mielikki teaches that nature and the works of mortals need not be enemies — that a village and the forest at its edge can flourish together, if both are treated with respect.

Her philosophy is one of compassionate stewardship. She loves all life — beast and bloom, predator and prey — and asks her faithful to walk lightly upon the earth, taking only what is needed and giving back more than they consume. She stands opposed to wanton destruction, whether it comes from the axes of loggers or the cruel sport of [[Malar]]'s hunters.

## Tenets
- Love all life and protect the balance between wilderness and civilisation.
- Take from the forest only what you need, and ensure it can regrow.
- Defend nature against those who would despoil it — [[Malar]]'s followers, careless fire mages, and reckless industry alike.
- Teach others the ways of sustainable living; knowledge shared is a forest planted.
- Walk through the woods with open ears. Listen before you act; protect before you strike.

## Followers & Influence
Rangers, woodsmen, and those who make their living at the boundary of settled land and deep forest revere Mielikki above all others. Her living temple, [[Mielikki's Grove]], stands in the [[Diamond District]] of [[Crystal City]] — a sanctuary where ancient trees form natural archways and stone blends seamlessly with living wood. The Grove houses the Healing Grove for the injured, the Wild Nursery for orphaned or wounded animals, and the Ranger's Rest for weary travellers.

[[Sylvara Oakwhisper]], a half-elf ranger, serves as a teacher and spiritual guide within the Grove. She has mentored [[Ember]], instructing them in Mielikki's ways: feeling the goddess's presence in the grass underfoot, learning to coexist with the wild, and understanding the threats posed by [[Malar]]'s followers. Sylvara's lessons emphasise that to follow Mielikki is to protect, to teach, and above all, to listen.`n
